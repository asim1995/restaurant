About Us

PROTEIN BAR Healthy Food Brand was founded in 2015

Our team has spent the last fest years immersed in learning the health foods industry and is committed to creating community, living sustainably and inspiring customers to have the highest standards in foods and products.

PROTEIN BAR is a healthy food brand aimed to cater to mass premium India. We Serve real food in every sense of the term, we do not add artificial ingredients, preservatives and are Organic. Our food & drink services has the right proportion of good fats, protein, vitamins, fibre and carbs recommended by nutritionists for all time meals.

Our dream is to make Protein Bar Food & Drinks available at every touch point in Indian life – be it a busy student, time-strapped professional or stay-at-home mother.
